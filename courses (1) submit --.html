Football Courses for Summer 2023

Offensive Play
    Learn the skills needed as a team to move the ball forward and past the oppostion in order to put the ball behind the net. Offensive play will also cover the art of dribbling and beating offside traps.
Defensive Play
    Back by popular demand! Learn what it takes to prevent the opposition from getting the ball past the team and into the net. You will learn how to tackle, press, and manmark the opposition efficiently.
Playmaker Passing
    You get to learn the basics of passing the ball efficiently to your teamates and around the opposition. Learn ground passes, through passses and lob passes to your team mates with the help of vison. You will also get to play against the Chelsea Football Club first team!
